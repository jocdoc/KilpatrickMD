Scope of Care

What I Treat

Comprehensive sports medicine bridged with thoughtful primary care — from diagnosis through return to sport.

01

Musculoskeletal & Injury Care

Acute and overuse injuries, tendinopathies, stress fractures, sprains, and return-to-sport clearance using evidence-based rehabilitation protocols.

02  ·  Specialty

Sport Concussion Management

As a concussion specialist with experience at the professional level, Dr. Kilpatrick provides comprehensive evaluation, baseline testing, symptom tracking, neuropsychological coordination, and individualized graduated return-to-play and return-to-learn protocols.

Leadership & Advisory

Beyond the Clinic

Dr. Kilpatrick's influence extends from the sideline to the boardroom — shaping sports medicine policy and athlete health at the organizational level.

NC Courage  ·  NWSL

Chief Medical Officer

As CMO of the NC Courage, Dr. Kilpatrick oversees all medical operations for one of the National Women's Soccer League's premier clubs. Her responsibilities span acute injury management, pre-season screening, concussion protocol oversight, travel medicine, and long-term athlete health strategy — all at the highest level of professional women's soccer.

Professional Sports NWSL Women's Soccer
Specialty

Sport Concussion Expert

Dr. Kilpatrick is a recognized expert in sport concussion — from sideline evaluation and baseline neurocognitive testing to return-to-play protocol design and concussion education for athletes, coaches, and organizations. She brings a research-informed, athlete-centered approach to one of sport's most complex clinical challenges.

Dr. Kilpatrick serves on multiple medical advisory boards and provides consulting services to sports organizations, health systems, and industry partners.
